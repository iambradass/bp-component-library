Meet Lisa

Lisa Morton has been a title rep for seven years in the Fort Worth market. She's good at her job — agents who work with her consistently rebook because she keeps transactions from falling apart and communicates at every step. She had built a solid referral base through relationships, lunches, and consistency.

But her growth had plateaued. She was closing about 85 transactions a year, and she could see the ceiling. The reps growing fastest in her market all had something she didn't: a professional web presence.

She'd thought about it for two years. She had a domain registered — it pointed to a blank parking page. She'd started a website on Squarespace and never finished it. She kept deprioritizing it because she didn't know where to start and didn't want to end up with something unprofessional.

"I kept telling myself I'd do it 'when things slowed down.' Things never slowed down. I realized I had to decide — or I'd still be saying the same thing in five years."

— Lisa Morton

What Happened: Week by Week

Lisa came in as a client in early February. Here's exactly what the process looked like:

Week 1
Discovery
30-min intake call + brand audit
We reviewed Lisa's current digital footprint, identified the gaps, and gathered everything needed — her story, her market, her differentiators. She spent 45 minutes total on her end.
Week 1–2
Build
Domain, website, and copy — built from scratch
We registered lisamortontitle.com, built a 6-page website with her story, service areas, lead capture, and SEO-optimized content. All copy written by us based on the intake call.
Week 2
Google
Google Business Profile configured
Set up, verified, and fully optimized her Google Business Profile — service areas, categories, description, photos, first 5 reviews strategically requested from existing clients.
Week 3
Launch
Site goes live — review sent, zero to do on Lisa's end
Lisa approved the final design in a 20-minute video call, we made two minor copy tweaks, and the site went live. Total time spent by Lisa: under 2 hours across 3 weeks.
Day 19
First Lead
First inbound inquiry arrives
"I got a form submission from a Keller Williams agent I'd never met. She found me on Google searching 'title rep Fort Worth.' That was the moment I knew this was real." — Lisa
Day 60
Results
14 monthly inbound leads, Page 1 Google ranking
