Questions you're probably asking

The questions we hear on every sales call — answered honestly.

Do I have to sign a long-term contract?
No contracts, ever. We're month-to-month. If you want to cancel, you give us 30 days notice and you're done. We keep clients by delivering results, not by locking them in. 94% of clients renew — that's the only number that matters to us.
How long until my site is live?
10 days on average, from your kickoff call to a fully live, Google-indexed website. We've done it in 7. The main bottleneck is usually getting your headshot and a 15-minute call with you to nail down the messaging.
What happens to my site if I cancel?
If you cancel, we take the site down at the end of your final billing period. You keep your domain name if you purchased one. If you want to keep the site and host it yourself, we can export it for a one-time fee. Either way, you're never left in a bad spot.
Who writes the content for my site?
We do. Our team writes all the copy based on a short intake form you fill out. You don't write a single word. We'll send you the draft for approval before anything goes live — most clients request only minor tweaks.
What if my broker objects to me having a personal site?
Most brokers are fine with it — it makes them look good too. If there are compliance concerns, we've navigated this before. We can adjust how the site is branded or positioned. We've never had a client have to take a site down because of broker policy.
How is this different from a template site?
Template sites use generic copy and shared domain patterns that Google doesn't rank well. We build custom sites with SEO-optimized content written for your specific market and name. There's a difference between a website that exists and one that gets found.
