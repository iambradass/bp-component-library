Annotated Diagram with Callout Pins

Numbered hotspot pins on a diagram. Click or hover any pin to reveal its explanation. Smart positioning — panels flip side if near the edge.

yourwebsite.com
Pin 1 — Navigation
Sticky Navigation Bar
Stays fixed at the top as users scroll. Includes logo, 3 nav links, and a CTA button. Mobile: collapses to a hamburger menu. Reduces bounce by 18% on average.
Pin 2 — Hero Section
Above-the-Fold CTA
First thing visitors see. Loaded in under 1 second. Headline answers "what you do + who for." One primary CTA button only — no decision paralysis. Critical for conversions.
Pin 3 — Social Proof
Trust-Building Logo Bar
Client/partner logos immediately below the hero section. "As seen in" style logos. Even 3 logos here increase conversion rate by an average of 12-15% in A/B tests.
Pin 4 — Feature Grid
Benefits Grid
3-column grid answering "why choose us." Each card: icon, benefit headline, 1–2 sentences. Addresses objections before they form. Use benefits (what they get), not features (what it does).
Pin 5 — CTA Section
Mid-Page Conversion Point
A second CTA for visitors who scrolled past the hero. Usually a softer ask: "Get a free audit" vs "Buy now." Catches the 35% of visitors who need more persuasion before converting.
Pin 6 — Footer
SEO Links & Contact Info
Footer links pass PageRank to important pages. NAP (Name, Address, Phone) helps local SEO. Privacy policy and terms links are legally required in most countries. Sitemap link helps crawlers.
