Step 03 / Mobile

52% of your visitors are on a phone

Mobile isn't an afterthought — it's the primary design surface. Every layout, tap target, and form field is tested on real devices before anything else.

  • 44px minimum touch targets — every button, link, and interactive element
  • Correct input types — numeric keyboards for phone fields, email for email
  • No horizontal overflow — tested on 320px viewport minimum
