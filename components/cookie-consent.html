Cookie Consent System

This page demonstrates a complete cookie consent implementation. The banner appears at the bottom, allows accepting all, rejecting all, or managing granular preferences. Your choice is saved to localStorage.

How it works

Accept or manage your cookie preferences using the bar at the bottom. After making a choice, the bar dismisses and a floating button appears in the corner to reopen settings at any time.

Categories

Four cookie categories: Strictly Necessary (always on), Analytics, Marketing, and Personalization. Each has a description and a cookie count.

Persistence

Preferences are saved to localStorage. Reload this page — the banner won't reappear until you reset via the floating button.
